MEDICAL VOLUNTEER

When my father opens a hospital, he decides on the principle of going for domestic medical service twice a year, donating briquettes once a year, and overseas service once a year with hospital staff members and my family. All the staff in the hospital volunteer to go for the service and half the cost of the service is covered by the hospital. The frame that my dad made for my birthday was absolutely amazing. The frame was made of the photos that I had volunteered and the frame is my treasure number 1 (1,2).
